Recipe
Saad's Specialty Chicken Tikka Pizza

Ingredients for my recipe

  • Pizza Dough,
  • Marinara Sauce,
  • Cheddar cheese,
  • Mozzarella cheese,
  • Chicken Tikka,
  • Onions,
  • Green Peppers,
  • Jalapeno
    • Recipe step by step
    1. Stretch your pizza dough out
    2. Add marinara sauce on your pizza
    3. add 2.0 ounces of mozzarella cheese
    4. add the chicken tikka to your pizza
    5. add a little green peppers and onions
    6. put jalapeno on half of the pizza
    7. put 1.5 ounces of cheddar cheese on top
    8. Leave it in the over for 10 mintues of 375 degress
