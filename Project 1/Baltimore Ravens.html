Fun Facts about the Baltimore Ravens

  • The balitmore ravens were founded in 1996
  • Two time super bowl champions
  • Two of the best players of all time played for the Ravens. Ray lewis and Ed Reed
  • The first player to ever be drafted by the Ravens went on to be a Hall of Famer.
  • Ravens Quarterback Lamar Jackson is the youngest signal caller in NFL history to start a playoff game
  • Balitmore Ravens are one of only two franchises to play in at least two Super Bowls and be undefeated
